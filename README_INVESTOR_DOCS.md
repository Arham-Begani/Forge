## **🔴 RED FLAGS TO AVOID**

Don't say:
- "We'll definitely reach $100M ARR" (unprovenunderpromise)
- "Competitors can't copy us" (they always can)
- "We have no weaknesses" (you look delusional)
- "This is the Uber of X" (investors hate comparisons)
- "We're raising to build in stealth mode" (wasted capital)

Do say:
- "Here's what could go wrong and how we're hedging"
- "We're building in public with beta feedback"
- "These are our hardest assumptions to prove"
